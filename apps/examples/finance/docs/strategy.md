## Boring on purpose

Monthly DCA into a global index core. Target **70% index / 20% crypto / 10% cash**,
crypto sleeve capped at **25% of net worth**, rebalance once a year.

- Automate the buys, ignore the noise.
- No leverage, no margin, no loans against crypto.
- Down-payment savings stay in short-term bonds, never in the risk sleeve.
